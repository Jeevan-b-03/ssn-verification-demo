Compliance & Usage

This page summarizes key compliance rules for Social Security Number verification integrations. Always consult official SSA documentation and your legal team.

SSNVS (Social Security Number Verification Service)

  • For wage reporting (Form W‑2) purposes only.
  • Verify up to 10 names/SSNs online or upload files (up to 250k) for next‑business‑day results.

Official page: ssa.gov/employer/ssnv.htm

eCBSV (electronic Consent Based SSN Verification)

  • API returns yes/no match and death indicator (if present).
  • Requires the SSN holder’s written consent (SSA‑89).
  • Limited to Permitted Entities (e.g., financial institutions and their service providers).

Overview: ssa.gov/dataexchange/eCBSV/

SSA‑89 (Written Consent)

  • One‑time use; typically valid for 90 days unless otherwise initialed by the signer.
  • Must include name, DOB, SSN, purpose, and signature (wet or electronic that meets requirements).

Form: ssa.gov/forms/ssa-89.pdf · Consent guidance: eCBSV Written Consent

Data Handling

  • Do not store plaintext SSNs. Store masked display + one‑way hash (or encrypt at rest) and enforce least privilege.
  • Keep consent artifacts and immutable audit logs for who/when/why each verification occurred.
